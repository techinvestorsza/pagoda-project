The Pagoda.

The legend stying and undeniable appeal of the 1968 Mercedes-Benz SL 'Pagoda' made the German two-seater popular at the time its availability in the 1960s, and its long-lasting charm has sustained demand as a coveted collector since then.

Pagoda production

Almost 49,000 Mercedes-Benz Pagoda cars were produced between 1963 and 1971, with nearly half the number destined for the USA. Today the Mercedes Benz Pagoda is a well established classic car, adored by collectors and enthusiasts alike.

Pagoda design team

The 'Pagoda' design team was led by Friedrich Geiger, yet its thought likely designer Paul Bracq and engineer Béla Baréhyi also had a big hand in the signed-off silhouette.
